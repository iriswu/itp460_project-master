I Think I'll Be Brave
MONDAY, OCTOBER 8, 2012

Yeah, not going to lie. That post about how I am able to quit my job without another lined up is just happening. Not now, anyway. Every waking moment I've had the past 10 days have been given to my wedding. The prep just never ends. I have my mom, dad, mother-in-law, godparents, and twin sister working around the clock and moving mountains to make this wedding happen. I am exhausted, spent, and don't know how much more I can give. I've doubted every decision, blurred the vision I had so clearly of how I want my wedding to be. It's been a roller coaster of highest highs and so many breakdowns. I am picking myself apart because the pressure to be perfect for my wedding day is just so great. Of course, this is all self-inflicted pressure. But still. After my last episode of tears, I decided I need to step away from wedding planning and write a blog post instead.

I just need to remember why I am doing this: To celebrate my marriage to the most incredible person in my life. I am marrying my best friend at the end of this month.

I think I'll be brave, starting with you.
