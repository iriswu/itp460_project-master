Do Not Play
MONDAY, OCTOBER 15, 2012

These days, my mind space is taken up by thoughts of tulle, paper flowers, tray-passed appetizers, and dance music. Yes, the wedding planning is still in full swing and it is my full-time job to ensure my most loved family and friends have the event of a lifetime.

I spend my days coordinating vendors, making countless trips to Michael's, and folding paper flowers until my fingers become so stiff I feel like I have arthritis. I come home with paper cuts all over my hands from the flowers and Lane complains that the wedding is so stressful. What will we ever do with the men?

Music has always been a huge part of my life so the music playlist has to be perfect (#firstworldproblems). But, I realized my "Do Not Play" list is ever growing:

  1. no Michael Jackson
  2. no Chicken Dance
  3. no Sweet Home Alabama
  4. no YMCA
  5. no Macarena
  6. no Jump On It
  7. no We Are Family
  8. NO Cupid Shuffle
  9. no Hoedown Throwdown (but YES to Party in the USA!)

Now I need your help: Where does Gangnam Style belong? Yes or no? Did I mention first world problems?
